We are excited to announce the opening of Wildwood Ranch!

Wildwood Ranch is located on 20 acres in Fall City, WA and offers camps where kids ages 5 to 13 can run wild and enjoy all that nature has to offer. Kids learn how to ride horses, cook on a campfire, make crafts, and just plain be kids.

MacKenzie and Kate Kinsman cut their teeth on summer camps for the past decade at Shoofly Farm. In recent years they have taken on more responsibilities, serving as interim-directors at times. Kate also spent two summers as a mountaineering guide for teens and adults in British Columbia.

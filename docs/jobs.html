We need a great team to pull off the magic of camp!

We run day camps 9am-3pm, Monday - Friday, for ages 5-13. Campers are rotated through activities such as crafts, snack, horses and games in groups with a counselor lead. The minimum age for employment is 16 years.

The 2024 camp season runs April 8-12, and June 17 - August 30th. Mandatory paid training is a hybrid of online and in-person, occuring in June prior to the start of camp.

Staff work hours are typically 8:30am - 3:30pm, Monday - Friday. We hire by the week, asking that staff commit to a minimum of three weeks per summer.

Our positions include:

  • Camp Counselor
  • Horse Wrangler
  • Lead Horse Wrangler
  • Activity Leader

If you think you'd be a good fit for any of our positions, apply for the 2024 season! The application will be available in February 2024. It is a very short application to collect basic information. The hiring process will consist of multiple interviews and references to gain a sense of each applicant's overall strengths in relation to camp rather than points on a resume.

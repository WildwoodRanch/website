Oh hey there

We are excited to announce the opening of Wildwood Ranch!

Wildwood Ranch is located on 20 acres in Fall City, WA and offers camps where kids ages 5 to 13 can run wild and enjoy all that nature has to offer. Kids will learn how to ride horses, cook on a campfire, make crafts, and have the freedom to just plain be themselves.

We (MacKenzie and Kate Kinsman) cultivated our passion for children and summer camp for the past decade at Shoofly Farm, at times serving as interim-directors. Kate also spent two summers as a mountaineering guide for teens and adults in British Columbia.
